Become an Activist!

Overview

Throughout this site, you have learned about famous female activists who fought hard for what they believed in. Today is a perfect time for you to follow in their footsteps! Whether you want to fight for gender equality or race equality, this is the tutorial for you. You will get inspired, get educated, and get involved in this easy-to-follow activism guide.

Materials

  • Water Bottle
  • Sign
  • Phone
  • Mask
  • A friend!

Step 1: Inspire Yourself!

Look back at a few of the women in the "Projects" tab. Why did they fight for their cause? What kinds of things did they do? Now take a look at few current activists. Here's a list to get you started. Some of these girls are probably your age or younger! How did they get started? Research the kinds of things they did to start making an impact on the world.

Step 2: Educate Yourself!

You should learn about some popular (and not so popular) activist movements! Feminism, LGBT rights, and the NAACP are always looking for help, just to name a few. Try to get in contact with a representive or branch in your area. Normally, these are listed on the website of the organization. You can follow this link to look up a few. Do you feel a connection with any of them? Do you have a reason to fight for that movement?

Step 3: Involve Yoursef!

Now that you've got some inspiration and you know how to become an activist, go out and do it! Create a sign, grab a water bottle, and go change the world! Invite a few friends to come with you to stand on a street corner.
