Become an Activist!

Overview

Throughout this site, you have learned about famous female activists who fought hard for what they believed in. Today is a perfect time for you to follow in their footsteps! Whether you want to fight for gender equality or race equality, this is the tutorial for you. You will get educated, get inspired, and get involved in this easy-to-follow activism guide.

Materials

  • Water Bottle
  • Sign
  • Phone
  • A friend!


Step 1: Educate Yourself!

You should learn about some popular (and not so popular) activist movements! Feminism, LGBT rights, and the NAACP are always looking for help, just to name a few. Try to get in contact with a representive or branch in your area. Normally, these are listed on the website of the organization. You can follow this link to look up a few.
